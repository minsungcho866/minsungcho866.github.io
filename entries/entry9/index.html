Miki Matsubara was a Japanese
composer, lyricist, and singer
from Nishi-ku, Sakai, Osaka, Japan.
She participated in the opening and
ending theme songs of the TV anime
"Gu-Gu Ganmo" under the name of

Reason Why I Chose This Song
This is a song by miki matsubara, a japanese singer-songwriter
who died in 2004. I knew this song 5 years ago and thought that it was
a song that was released recently. However, after knowing that the
singer of this song was dead when I was 1 years old and the song was
written way before that, I was so amazed and it made me to get interested
to this song. I started listening to this song again recently because while
I was searching for a song to listen to, I saw is song and it reminded
me of the time when I enjoyed listening to this song. So, I started
listening to it and was amazed about how elegant and modern this song
was. Therefore, I chose this song for the song of this week.
